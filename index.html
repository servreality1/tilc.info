What is the process of developing a crypto game?

The possibility of developing crypto games today is just beginning to emerge and the process of creating such games is not as complicated as it may seem at first glance.

To begin with, the idea of game development and its mechanics should be defined. This has a great contribution to the process of game development later on, as it allows you to clarify what needs to be done next.

As a next step, it is worthwhile to take the creation of a plan of the necessary work and how much time it will take to implement each task in a quality way. This way in the development process does not waste time on less important activities that are not included in the work plan.
